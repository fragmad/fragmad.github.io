Finally!

I have finally finished a version of my site’s theme which I’m happy with. It isn’t anything special and it isn’t anything fancy, but it’s mine, so falls firmly into the if I break it I fix it way of things, which is exactly what I want this site.

The week I’d intended to spend working on the theme was interrupted by working on another site I manage and then week on-call. Then some other weeks where stuff happened. Anyway, I’m on the second day of a three day weekend and managed to find the half hour required to tinker with the theme so it now works. This is good and I’ll be happy to publish more soon.
