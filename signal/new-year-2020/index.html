New Year 2020

Wed Jan 1, 2020

Happy New Year!

Thank God the old year is over.

I do not intend to write a end of year wrap up as I did last year. Public reflection isn't a genre of writing I enjoy contributing to even if I'm a happy voyer of other's contributions, so this post, as reflective as it is, is in my semi-secret feed.

So last year:

  • I didn't like the UK election results one bit and I'm not hopeful for the future
  • I moved to Scotland
  • I started working as a remote first employee
  • I briefly fell out of love to varying degrees with writing anything either code or prose
  • I started rock climbing again and rediscovered a central metaphor in my life
  • In the last couple of weeks of the year I started free-writing on a daily basis again

I can't predict what will happen in the coming year, but I can describe some aspirations:

  • I'd like to regularly lead climbing sport routes at 6c/7a by the end of the year
  • I want to continue free-writing and discover a fluency with the written word that either I lost or never possessed
  • I should nap more
  • My career and professional development has been in a holding pattern for a couple of years, and I can really do better than I am now
