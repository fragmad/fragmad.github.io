Tue Feb 11, 2020

The sun is setting and I have been toiling away doing some tasks which should be automated for the #dayjob. In about half an hour I’m heading to my local climbing wall to see what they have planned for the year. I don’t know what to expect from Transition Extreme since I’m a relatively new customer of theirs, but I’d like to see the bouldering area enlarged.

One of my fingers slightly hurts from a finger board session on Sunday, so I won’t be climbing tonight. There’s a social climbing event on Friday and a potential, if the weather holds, trip to a quarry about forty minutes north of me for some sport climbing, and I’d like to be in good nick for those.

Oh, I ran my second complete 5K yesterday with a faster time.

I’m reading William Gibson’s Agency very slowly.

Will Ellwood,
11 Feb 2020,
Aberdeen
